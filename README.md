# Market

Market is an iOS application that transforms your device into a powerful marketplace, combining food and grocery delivery in one seamless platform. Users can browse, order, and manage their shopping with ease, thanks to its intuitive interface, Firebase integration, and real-time data syncing.

## Features

### General
- **Product & Food Browsing**: Navigate a rich catalog of groceries and restaurant menus with categorized listings.
- **Dynamic Cart Management**: Add, remove, and adjust quantities of items with real-time updates.
- **Personalized Suggestions**: Smart recommendations based on cart content or browsing history.

### Account Management
- **User Authentication**: Sign up, log in, and manage your account securely with Firebase Authentication.
- **Profile Customization**: Update personal details effortlessly.

### Address Management
- **Map Integration**: Add and manage delivery addresses using MapKit and Core Location.
- **Saved Addresses**: Store multiple addresses with titles for easy selection during checkout.

### Food & Grocery Delivery
- **Seamless Food Ordering**: Browse and order meals from various restaurants, including full menu support.
- **Grocery Shopping**: Add multiple items to your cart from the grocery section, including support for special instructions.

### Data & Image Management
- **Cloud Storage**: All user data, including products and addresses, is securely stored in Firebase Firestore.
- **Image Hosting**: Product and food images are dynamically fetched from Firebase Storage for high-quality visuals.

## How to Use

### Getting Started
1. **Sign Up or Log In**: Create an account using your email and password or log in to an existing account.
2. **Explore Products and Food**: Browse through categories to find items or meals you want to order.

### Address Management
1. **Add New Addresses**: Use the integrated map view to select a location or manually enter address details.
2. **Update or Delete Addresses**: Easily manage your saved addresses for future orders.

### Ordering
1. **Select Items**: Add groceries or food items to your cart.
2. **Checkout**: Review your cart and delivery address, and confirm the order.
